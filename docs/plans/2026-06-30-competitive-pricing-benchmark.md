# Competitive Pricing Benchmark — AI Governance Category

**Date:** 2026-06-30
**Status:** Research complete — pricing locked at £129 / £499 / Custom (Rob confirmed 2026-06-30 post-review)
**Related:** [`2026-06-30-value-slice-pricing.md`](./2026-06-30-value-slice-pricing.md)

## Headline

**Verisum is radically underpriced.** Every enterprise competitor starts at **$30K–$50K/yr entry** (~£2,000–£3,300/mo). Only three players sit near Verisum's price band: Modulos (free Starter, 1 user), Fiddler (free guardrails + $0.002/trace), and Vireo Sentinel ($55/mo for 5 users). At £79/£199 Verisum is pricing like a hobby tool while shipping enterprise-grade capabilities.

**Nobody else has chain-anchoring.** That is a category-of-one moat currently uncaptured in Verisum's pricing.

## Where Verisum sits (before repricing)

- **Core £79/mo (~$100)** — one of very few *published* prices in the category. Only Modulos Free and Fiddler Free undercut you; Vireo ($55) is close. Everyone else starts $30K+/yr (£2,000+/mo). **Radically below market**, giving away price anchor authority.
- **Assure £199/mo (~$250)** — still an order of magnitude below the next real competitor. Mid-market buyers assume "toy tool" at this level.
- **Verify custom** — standard for the category, appropriate.

## Recommended pricing options

### Option A — "Match published floor" (safe, minor uplift)
- **Core £99 / Assure £299 / Verify custom**
- Justification: £99 crosses the psychological SME threshold; £299 signals mid-market seriousness; still cheapest published enterprise-grade option.

### Option B — "Value-slice premium" (research-recommended)
- **Core £129 / Assure £399 / Verify from £1,500/mo (or custom)**
- Rationale: every tier now includes govern + monitor + prove slices, so uplift is justified by MORE capability. £129 still 20× cheaper than Credo/Holistic; £399 still 5× cheaper than OneTrust entry. Verify anchoring is a category-of-one — publishing a "from" floor creates a discoverable anchor while leaving Enterprise custom.

### Option C — "Signal enterprise" (aggressive)
- **Core £149 / Assure £499 / Verify from £2,500/mo**
- Rationale: matches Vireo's per-user maths at ~10-user teams; forces mid-market conversation, not SME race-to-bottom. Risk: kills self-serve conversion from Explorer.

## Rob's final decision (2026-06-30)

**£129 Core / £499 Assure / Verify custom (no published floor)**

- Core matches Option B (£129) — the research-recommended value-slice premium.
- Assure matches Option C (£499) rather than Option B (£399) — creates bigger headroom before Verify, positions Assure firmly in mid-market approval territory rather than SME impulse-buy.
- Verify stays fully custom (Rob's preference over publishing a £1,500 floor) — retains negotiation flexibility per deal.
- Annual discount: 20% off (£1,238/year Core, £4,790/year Assure).

## Pricing psychology notes

- **£99 > £79** — under-£100 ceiling doesn't signal "hobby tool" (worth ~15% conversion uplift in SME SaaS)
- **£129 > £99** — small increment for meaningful value-add, still under £150 psychological ceiling
- **Charm pricing** (.99 not .00) — category norm; use £129 not £130, £399 not £400
- **20% annual discount** — Vireo publishes 25%, competitors 15-20%
- **Flat pricing, not per-user** — governance buyer wants to invite auditors/legal/execs as viewers; per-user pricing (OneTrust, Vireo) punishes that
- **Anchor the moat in copy** — "on-chain cryptographic proof" as included in Verify is a category-of-one claim; that earns the custom-price positioning
